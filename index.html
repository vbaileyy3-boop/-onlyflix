What is OnlyFlix

OnlyFlix is a free streaming catalog where you can watch the latest movies and TV shows in the best possible quality — no registration, no subscription. Metadata and posters are live from TMDB; the player supports direct MP4, adaptive HLS (.m3u8) and third-party embed sources.

» The process is very simple

Pick a title, hit Play, and the built-in player loads instantly. Switch source/server, quality, subtitles, or playback speed on the fly.

» Privacy and data

No registration required. We don't store personal data. Cookies are only used to remember your watch history, kept locally in your browser.

» OnlyFlix and VPN

No regional restrictions and VPN-agnostic accessibility — works the same wherever you are.

» Source resolver

Each title resolves to MP4 / HLS / embed sources via a pluggable registry. HLS plays through hls.js with native Safari fallback.
